Pacinvaders: A Retro Arcade Experience

Enemies

The enemies are now represented as space invaders style aliens, enhancing the retro feel of the game.

Sound Effects

We've improved the sound effects! The screaming has been removed and replaced with retro arcade sounds for a more authentic experience.

Level Progression

Experience a revamped level progression system that gradually increases in difficulty, providing a challenging experience as players advance through the game.

Skill Tree & Ability Upgrades

Players can now customize their gameplay through a skill tree that allows for unique ability upgrades, enhancing the overall gaming experience.

Boss Designs

We have introduced better boss designs to increase excitement during boss fights, making them more memorable encounters.

Overall Game Polish

The game has undergone significant polishing to improve visuals, gameplay, and player engagement.
